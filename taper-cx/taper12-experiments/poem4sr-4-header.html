“Title” by Author

VoiceOver: a poem for screen readers

  1. Activate screen reader: press Command + F5
  2. Open VoiceOver rotor: Caps Lock + U
  3. Press ⬇ to select main
  4. Enter / Return
  5. Read all: Caps Lock + A

You want to read?

I want to read

You must follow instructions to the letter

Will you learn my commands?

You sense something missing

Who speaks for me?

You squint at the screen

Don't leave me unspoken.

Will you make the effort to hear me?

A voice that is not mine speaks my thoughts

Do you know where you are?
