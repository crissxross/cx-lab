A poem for screen readers

VoiceOver says...

Narrator says...

The voice in your head, who is it?

Your inner Narrator

Inner critic or inner ally?

The VoiceOver for your life

The Narrator of your life

Who speaks your thoughts?

Who speaks?

Will you make the effort to hear me?

Or leave me unspoken?

The spoken unspoken

Who speaks the picture?

How do you picture what's said?

Hear the silent words

Make the effort

Listen to the unspoken
